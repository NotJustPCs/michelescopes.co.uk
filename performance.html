About me

I am an experienced and versatile violinist; I have performed in events ranging from playing on tour with Katherine Jenkins and Blake to Solo Recitals, Weddings, Corporate functions and Session work. In a more intimate environment I can play in a solo, duo or string quartet. The Elle Duo and Salo Quartet are flexible and fun bringing an extraordinary atmosphere to any event.

Elle Duo

Elle Duo is a combination of two violinists. Our combination creates the perfect atmosphere for smaller environments, and a delicate and sophisticated feel to any event. We are happy to play at any occasion from Weddings, Corporate Functions, Parties, Private dinners and many more. Listen to audio of our arrangements below.

Salo Quartet

The Salo Quartet is an all female string quartet that prides itself on providing a professional and personal service.

We are based in the South East and have played at many venues and events including:

  • The Barbican Centre
  • Anthony Nolan Trust Fund Summer Ball
  • Theatre openings
  • Boat Trips on the Thames
  • Weddings at Buxted Park Hotel
  • The Grand Brighton
  • Wickwoods Country Club
  • Glynde House
  • Richmond Park House
  • Brighton Pavilion

and many more...

We have a delightful repertoire taken from both classical and informal music providing great entertainment for every event. Our repertoire is continually growing and we will happily help you to select the music for your occasion. The Salo quartet play at any number of events from weddings, corporate functions, parties, and formal dinners, to name a few.

Please contact me to discuss your personal occasion further and for information on our fees.
